We help secure the future !

We offer the following services

Multidimensional server penetration testing

To make sure that the servers that you are running your services on are secure enough, we will perform multiple deep footprinting and reconnaissance techniques before starting to penetrate through your system. You could use our 'Server penetration testing' service to test the security of your web servers, DNS servers, SMTP, cloud, etc etc.

Web application penetration testing

If you want to find out about any underlying vulnerability in your web-app that could put your website in danger, we provide web application penetration testing services which involves trying various automated and manual web application attacks.

Social engineering and exploitation

If you can communicate with it, you can hack it, and yes! People are included too! If you want to find out whether your employees could compromise your business by not being cautious enough, we provide social engineering and phishing services, since these kinds of attacks are becoming increasingly common.

Secure app developement

We provide full-stack web development services at a very professional level. While we are developing our webapps, our main concern is to make them as secure and well functioning as possible by using various secure coding techniques. Each app that we make is provided with a full vulnerability assessment to be sure that we clear out as many security flaws as humanly possible.

So who are we ?

We are a team of developers who are mainly concerned with cybersecurity and with writing secure code. We believe that any public app or service of any form should be checked for potential vulnerabilities that could cost the deployer resources and benefit the potential hacker. However no one knows whether their code is fully secure or not until they get hacked. All of our services have one aim, which is to create a technological future where cybersecurity is not a major concern for you.
